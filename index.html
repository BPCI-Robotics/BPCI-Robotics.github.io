Birchmount Robotics Club

About Us

The Birchmount Robotics Club is the robotics club of Birchmount Park Collegiate Institute in Scarborough, ON. Our members are experienced in various fields, such as computer-aided design and programming. Our club is completely student-run, and everything is managed by the members of the club.

Our club is mostly comprised of students in the gifted program. This year, our new members are already proving very helpful. With our new motivation, as well as our previous experience, we are excited to compete at the competitions.
